Our Mission
We, the team of Akshar, dream of an India, where everyone has the opportunity to get educated without depending on how much money they have. Our team is continuously working to find out the talents from every conrners our country and nourish them. We aspire every minds of India, to be the part of this initiative our realising our dream.
Our Story
We have witnessed the unprecedented outbreak of Covid-19 virus, which took the toll of a huge number of lives. Apart from these, it has affected all the aspects of human life. But the situation of the stranded unpriviledged people is even worse. They didn't have enough food, clothes, medicines or proper hygiene. In such a situation educating them is a Herculean task. That is why, we thought to form a team and work for educating them, besides, arranging a better, healthy rather a beautiful life to them.
